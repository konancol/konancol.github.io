Overview

Minecraft VS Zombies 2 (MVZ2 for short) is a fan game of Minecraft, Plants vs. Zombies, and Touhou Project series, created by Cuerzor alone.

The game also features the Plants vs. Zombies game playing, elements of Minecraft, and places and characters from Touhou Project series, in which players take on the role of Steve the mechanic as he travels with his friend Crazy Villager to Gensokyo to solve the Minecraft Incident.

There are two versions of the game. The previous version made with Gamemaker Studio 2 was updated to chapter 4 and is now obsoleted. The current Unity version are in development, is expected to have around 112 contraptions, 64 enemies and 132 adventure levels.

For the latest news about the game, please pay attention to the creator Cuerzor's online news, as well as the game's change log.

Game Contents

ME

ME(Mechanical Energy) is a resource that must be used to place the contraptions, corresponding to Sun in Plants vs. Zombies.

If you have no ME, you cannot battle with enemies. Additional ME can be obtained by collecting redstones, or by using ME production contraptions.

You can only have at most 9,990 ME at a time.

Contraptions

Contraptions are the tools you use to attack your enemies, corresponding to Plants in Plants vs. Zombies.

There are many different kinds of contraptions, such as:

  • Dispenser for attacking enemies.
  • Furnace for producing redstone.
  • Obsidian for blocking enemies.
  • Mine TNT for blowing up enemies.
  • So on.

The specified ME must be spent to place contraptions. After you place a contraption, you must wait a certain amount of recharging time before you can place the contraption again. The recharging time is different for each device.

Enemies

Enemies are what you need to defeat, corresponding to Zombies Plants vs. Zombies.

There are various kinds of enemies, such as:

  • Zombie which is the most common.
  • Iron Helmet Zombie which is tougher.
  • Skeleton which shoots arrows to your contraptions.
  • So on.

The core of the game is how to defeat enemies intelligently with the contraptions.

Gems

Gems are collectable objects that corresponding to Coins in Plants vs. Zombies.

There are three types of gems:

  • Emeralds worth 10.
  • Rubies worth 50.
  • Diamonds worth 1000.

After you have collected enough gems, you can buy and unlock items from events or the store to increase your power.

Battlefields

Battlefields are sites for battling. Different battlefields have different effects.

For example: Halloween is at night time, so you can't get additional redstones from the solar mine, and will summon two gargoyle statues then disrupt your formation at each huge wave of enemies.

Starshards

Starshards are powerful resources, corresponding to Plant Foods in Plants vs. Zombies 2.

After you defeat an enemy that emits green light, it will drop a starshard. Or you can get additional starshards by starshard producing contraptions.

When using the starshard on a contraption, the contraption is evoked by the starshard and erupts with a power enough to change the outcome of the battle.

Different contraptions have different evoked abilities, and using starshards wisely can reduce the difficulty of the game.

Almanac

Almanac is an in-game item that allows you to read additional information by viewing it after you unlock it.

The Almanac includes the evoked ability of the contraptions, as well as the specific data of enemies and the contraptions. You can also see the characters here, as well as a few miscellaneous items.

Bosses

Bosses are the goals of each chapter in Adventure mode. You must defeat the boss to unlock the next chapter.

Each boss has great power, which is a big challenge for players. However, they can be easily solved after found their weaknesses.

Difficulties

The Difficulties option is a new system in this game, with different levels of difficulty.

There are three difficulties:

  • Easy
  • Normal
  • Hard

For the differences between difficulties, see the Difficulty Differences entry.

FAQ

Q: Why don't you answer me when I ask you a question?

A：The creator will only answer questions that do not exist on this page.

For those problems that already exist on this page, no anwsers will be left if asked.

Q: How do I change the language?

A:

  1. After entering the mainmenu, input the user name and click the button on the left to confirm (确认).
  2. Click the wooden sign on the left to open the menu of Options (选项).
  3. Click the button on the bottom left to open More options (更多选项...).
  4. Click on the Language (语言) drop-down box at the top, and then select the language you want.
  5. Click the bottom button "Back" (返回) to close more options.
  6. click the bottom right button "Back" (返回) to close the options menu.
  7. Now you successfully changed the language.

Q: What if there is a BUG?

A: You can send an email to the creator's mailbox: mvz2feedback@qq.com.

Please explain the problems you have encountered in the description.

A game log file can be included if necessary. the path is %HOMEPATH%/AppData/LocalLow/Tocmic Studio/MinecraftVSZombies2, the files are named Player.log and Player-prev. Log (log file created when you started the game at the last time).

Q: Where's the path of my save files?

A: "%HOMEPATH%/AppData/LocalLow/Tocmic Studio/MinecraftVSZombies2/saves" for Windows,

"/storage/emulated/0/Android/data/com.tocmic.mvz2/files/saves" for Android.

Difficulty Differences

Easy

General

  • The recharge time of blueprint has been reduced to 80%.
  • A redstone gives 50 points of ME.
  • The spawn rate of starshard zombies has been increased.
  • After clearing a level, only 2 rubies will be given if it's already cleared, and the remaining carts will be settled as emeralds.

Contraptions

  • Moonlight Sensors' yield is doubled.
  • The light radius of contraptions increased by 1 cell length.
  • Triggering contraptions costs 0 ME.

Enemies

  • The toughness of Enemies' armors is halfed.
  • Parasitized enemies by Mother Terror will only spawn 2 Parasite Terrors.

Bosses

  • Frankenstein will not transform into phase 2.
  • Slenderman will do 3 Fate Choosing at most, and has 4 options.
  • Nightmareaper's Crushing Walls' speeds are slower.
  • Nightmareaper's time limit is 2 minutes.

Misc

  • The count of ghosts in Whack-A-Ghost Minigame is reduced by 25%.

Hard

General

  • No carts.
  • After clearing a level, a diamond will be given if it's already cleared, or 5 rubies will be given.

Enemies

  • Enemies's attack damage increased by 50%.
  • The healing and damage of Mummies' gas is doubled.
  • Parasitized enemies by Mother Terror will spawn 4 Parasite Terrors.
  • Mother Terror will spawn 2 Parasite Terrors upon death with eggs.

Bosses

  • Frankenstein will transform into phase 2 as soon as it appears.
  • Slenderman will do 5 Fate Choosing at most, and has only 2 options.
  • Slenderman will transform blueprints into zombies, and zombie blueprints will not be transformed.
  • Nightmareaper's Crushing Walls' speeds are faster.
  • Damage of Nightmareaper's Wheel of Death is doubled.
  • Nightmareaper's time limit is 1 minute.

Misc

  • The punishment time of whacking Napstablooks is increased to 2s from 1s in Whack-A-Ghost Minigame.
